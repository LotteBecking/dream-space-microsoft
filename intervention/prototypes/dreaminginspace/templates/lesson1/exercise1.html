💡 What is Sequencing?

Sequencing means putting steps in the right order. In computing, if you put instructions in the wrong order, you get the wrong result!

Think about getting dressed in the morning:

✅ Right order
Step 1: Put on your socks
Step 2: Put on your shoes
👍 This makes sense!
❌ Wrong order
Step 1: Put on your shoes
Step 2: Put on your socks
😬 That doesn't work!

The steps below for making tea are all mixed up. Your job is to drag them into the right order!
