💡 What makes a good algorithm?

An algorithm is a set of step-by-step instructions. Think of it like a recipe — if you miss a step or put them in the wrong order, things go wrong! Each step should be one clear action that even a robot with no common sense can follow.

🔍 See an example
❌ Bad: "Make a sandwich"
✅ Good:
  1. Open the bread bag
  2. Take out two slices of bread
  3. Place one slice on the plate
  4. Open the peanut butter jar
  5. Pick up the knife
  6. Spread peanut butter on the bread

Tip from Space Beaver

Write like a robot reads it

Remember: a robot has NO common sense! Every action needs its own step.

3 Rules:
1️⃣ Each step = one action
2️⃣ Be specific (not vague)
3️⃣ Use action verbs (open, pour, pick up...)
Bad vs Good
❌ "Make breakfast"
✅ "Open the cereal box"
✅ "Pour cereal into bowl"
✅ "Open the fridge"
✅ "Take out the milk"
