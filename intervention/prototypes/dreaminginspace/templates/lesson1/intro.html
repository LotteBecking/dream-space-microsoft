💡 What is an Algorithm?

An algorithm is just a fancy word for a set of step-by-step instructions that tell someone (or something!) exactly what to do.

You already use algorithms every day without even knowing it!

🍳
Following a recipe
🧱
Building LEGO
🗺️
Directions to school
🎮
Game instructions

⚠️ Important: Computers and robots follow instructions EXACTLY. They can't guess what you mean! If your instructions are wrong or unclear, the output will be wrong too.
