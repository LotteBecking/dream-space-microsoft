💡 How to Write a Loop

When you see the same steps repeated, you can wrap them in a LOOP. A loop has two parts: how many times to repeat, and what steps to repeat.

LOOP [number] TIMES: [steps that repeat]

Look at the long code below. Spot the repeating section and pick the correct loop count!
