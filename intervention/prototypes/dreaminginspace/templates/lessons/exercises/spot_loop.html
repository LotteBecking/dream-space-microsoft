💡 What is a Loop?

A loop is when you repeat the same steps over and over again. Instead of writing "clap" ten times, you can say "LOOP 10 TIMES: clap"!

❌ Without a loop:
Jump, Jump, Jump, Jump, Jump
5 lines of code!
✅ With a loop:
LOOP 5 TIMES: Jump
Just 1 line!
