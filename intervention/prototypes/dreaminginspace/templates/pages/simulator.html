🤖 What are Instructions?

Computers and robots can only understand very specific instructions. They have no common sense — they can't guess what you mean!

In this simulator, you'll give a robot instructions to make a PB&J sandwich. Use the buttons below to add steps, then press Run to see if the robot can follow them!
